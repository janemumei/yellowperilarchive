Commentary

From “Red Dawn” to SARS scares, Chinese hackers to Saturday Night Live yellowface, the lens of yellow perilism continues to distort the modern, normative “Western” worldview. Here, a select group of scholars, students, and activists share fresh analysis of outbreaks of Yellow Peril, past and present.
